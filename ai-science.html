AI Agents

Revolutionizing scientific research through artificial intelligence and machine learning

Overview of AI agent

An AI agent is a computational system that autonomously integrates prior knowledge, data, and algorithms to reason, make decisions, and take actions toward a scientific goal. In our lab, AI agents synthesize literature and datasets, perform analysis and modeling, and iteratively guide experimental design and interpretation through feedback from experimental results.
