Wang Lab

Virus-Immune System Coevolution

Bridging experimental biology with artificial intelligence to understand and predict the dynamic coevolution between viruses and immune systems. Our lab pioneers high-throughput methods, AI-driven research, and adaptive feedback loops to tackle emerging viral threats.

Our Mission

To revolutionize our understanding of virus-immune system interactions through the integration of cutting-edge experimental methods and artificial intelligence. We aim to create predictive models that can anticipate viral evolution and guide the development of next-generation therapeutics and vaccines.
